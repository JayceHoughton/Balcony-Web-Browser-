Commonly Asked Questions

To create a panel, click anywhere on the canvas with the mouse and drag down and to the right. Once an empty black rectangle appears and it is the panel is at the desired size, let go of the mouse.

To move a panel, move your mouse cursor towards the top left hand corner of the panel until your cursor turns into a four directional arrow. When that appears, click your mouse and drag the panel where you want.

To resize a panel, move your mouse cursor towards the bottom right hand corner of the panel until your cursor turns into a two-sided diagonal arrow. Then, move your mouse until the panel is about the size you want it. Let go of the mouse to finalize the resized.

To delete a panel, move your mouse cursor to the top right hand corner of the panel you want to delete. The cursor will not change, but if you click in the top right hand corner of a panel, the panel will be deleted.

If you are using a guest account, your panels aren't stored between uses. You would need to upgrade your account a premium account. If you are a premium user and your panels are resetting when you attempt to move or resize them, you cannot put panels on top or within other panels to prevent conflicts. If your panels continue to reset when you reopen your program, you may need to download your software again.
